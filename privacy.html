Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

RelayOps is a dispatch management platform for Amazon Relay operators. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when using RelayOps:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password
  • Driver information: names, phone numbers, and email addresses you add to your roster
  • Location data: GPS coordinates from drivers using the app during active shifts
  • Usage data: dispatch activity, messages, alerts, and reports you create
  • Device information: device type and operating system for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the RelayOps platform
  • Enable real-time driver tracking and dispatch management
  • Send driver invitation emails via SendGrid
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Respond to support requests and communications
  • Improve and maintain the app

Location Data

RelayOps collects GPS location data from drivers only when the app is in use during active shifts. This data is used solely to display driver locations to dispatch managers on the live map. Location data is stored securely in Firebase and is only accessible to the manager who manages that driver.

Drivers can stop sharing location at any time by closing the app.
